Informational Card — grey box

surface #f1f0ee border 1px #e4e2dd heading navy #0a2540 eyebrow #1e88e5

Not a content block — a structural pattern documented in house-style.md, shown here for visual reference beside the navy and soft cards. Wraps explanatory or instructional prose (a report overview, a “how to leverage this” passage) as its own section; the navy heading ties it to the navy-box family. Often used as a pair (overview + how-to) with a 20px gap between cards. Don’t use it for clickable lists (Linked Session List) or ranked points (Numbered Outcome Grid). Example: Q2-2026-GigaOm-Active-Partners.html.

Format · Personal outreach · spec in plain-text-style.md

Plain-Text Email — logo + prose + links

Background: white, no card Images: logo only One CTA: bold link, no button Kind: Plain-Text Outreach

The whole sibling format, not a block: survey asks, rep outreach, reminders, low-key notes that should read like a one-to-one email. No hero, cards, buttons, or section patterns — bold only on the lead-in and the CTA, at most one emoji pointer, and the {{#if Sender.Name}} signature conditional with company fallback. Shown: the spec’s filled ARC-reminder example (CTA destination per send). Log these with kind Plain-Text Outreach.
